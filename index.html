About Us

We are a group of young developers from Milwaukee and Chicago with particular interests in problem solving. We possess a diverse combined background and knowledge that we have applied to specific problems in Medical Imaging. Thanks to our many sponsors and mentors, we have been able to attend the Society of Imaging Informatics in Medicine's Annual Meetings. Through the SIIM hackathon, we have applied our combined skill sets to challenge existing solutions.
